I am an Associate Researcher in the Department of Automation, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I received my Ph.D. from USTC in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Zheng-Jun Zha and Prof. Yang Cao, and my B.S. degree from Southwest Jiaotong University in 2017. My research focuses on Embodied Perception and Interaction, Multimodal Reasoning and Generation, and Neuromorphic Vision. I was fortunate to receive the AAAI 2023 Distinguished Paper Award and the ACM MM 2025 MSMA Workshop Best Student Paper Award.
